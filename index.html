KeebParts.eu

A collection of keyboard part stores within the EU to safely shop without worrying about customs and import taxes.

I have not bought from all of these stores and thus cannot verify their legitimacy. Do your own research before buying.

General

CandyKeys European Store selling popular keyboards, keycap sets and components (ISO + ANSI). Based in Germany.
KeyGem Switches, Keycaps, lubes, cables, DIY kits and deskmats. Based in Germany, also ships worldwide.
Oblotzky Industries Group buys, keycaps, limited items such as switches, deskmats and keyboard bags in stock. Based in Germany.
CoffeeKeys Italian keyboard store shipping worldwide.
Eloquent Clicks Store based in Spain. Keyboards, cases, keycaps, switches, stabilizers, PCBs and other accessories. Group buys as well.
Shelter Poland based shop providing keyboards and desk accessories. Selling popular brands like Keychron, Ducky, NuPhy, etc.
salvun Located in Belgium. Artisan and keyboard maker. Offers keyboard preorders and parts.
keebwerk German based keyboard maker with focus on industrial design. Also selling keyboard parts.
Centorek Based in Poland, selling Krytox and TriboSys lubricants, keyboard parts, tools and other accesories.
Delta Key Selling a wide variety of switches (including lubed) as well as keyboards, keycaps and other accessories. Located in France.
Monaco Keys Selling keyboards, keycaps, switches and more based in Germany.
Clickeys Selling budget friendly keyboards and kits as well as switches, keycaps and more. Located in the Netherlands.
MechMarket Trade and sell keyboards and keyboard parts. Filter by EU tagged posts.

Split/Ergonomic Keyboards

42Keebs Mostly PCBs, tools and accessories. Located in Czech Republic.
splitkb What's in a name: sells split keyboards and has accessories. DIY focused. Netherlands based.

(ISO) Keycaps

Teleport Manufacturer of custom keyboards and ISO DE keycaps based in Germany.
